This summer, I’m planning a small trip in Europe. I want to visit some friends, workplaces, and interesting people. My goal is to work on some of my pet projects and to meet people in person.

## Questions for this season

- Font-Editor – What is the current status of font editors in Europe, and why is Europe dominating the font editor market?
- How to Learn in 2026 – What are the best ways to learn new skills and knowledge in a world where AI is becoming more prevalent?
- Beowolf – Research more about the font Beowolf and learn about its history.

## Goals for this season

- Visit at least 5 interesting places in Europe related to typography and and learning.
- Meet at least 10 interesting people in person and have meaningful conversations with them.
- Have a first draft of a Beowolf publication ready by the end of the season.

## Context

Over 15 years ago, I did a tour in the US with Michi. We traveled and worked on some projects with local people and companies. We had a lot of fun and learned a great deal. We also had some wonderful experiences with the people we met and the places we visited. [→ see Project Website](https://caminantes-grafico.org/). This season continues that spirit of exploration and learning, but with a focus on specific questions I have.
